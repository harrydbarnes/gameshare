PS5 Game Sharing Setup Guide

Enter Player Names

Instructions for Player 1 (Game Owner)

On Player 2's PS5, Player 1 logs in with their PSN ID.

  1. Go to Settings > Users and Accounts > Other > Console Sharing and Offline Play.
  2. Select Enable.
    • If it says "Disable", it's already enabled for another account. Only one account per PS5 can enable this.
    • If it says "Don't Disable", Player 1's account has this enabled on another PS5. Disable it there first.
  3. Once enabled, Player 1 should log out of their account on Player 2's PS5 (Settings > Users and Accounts > Other > Sign Out).

Summary for Player 1: On Player 2's PS5, log in, enable Console Sharing, then log out.

Instructions for Player 2 (Game Receiver)

Once Player 1 has finished:

  1. Log into your own PSN account on your PS5.
  2. Go to your Game Library.
  3. Player 1's games will appear in "Your Collection".
  4. Download and play!

Summary for Player 2: Player 1's games are now in your library.

Key Points & Risks

  • Trust is Crucial: Player 1 logs into their account on Player 2's PS5. Share only with trusted individuals. Use 2-Step Verification on Player 1's account.
  • Primary PS5: Player 1 makes Player 2's PS5 their primary console for "Console Sharing and Offline Play." This means Player 1 needs internet to play their own digital games on their own PS5.
  • Digital Games Only: Does not work for disc games.
  • Account Safety: Account sharing is against PSN Terms of Service. Risk is low but not zero.
  • DLC Shared: DLC and add-ons are usually shared.

After Setup

  • Player 1 (Owner): Needs internet to play their digital games on their own PS5.
  • Player 2 (Receiver): Can play shared games offline on their PS5.
  • Playing Together: Both can play online (different games or same shared game) on their own accounts.
  • Don't Share Player 1's Account Login: Player 2 plays on their *own* account.
  • Changing Primary: If Player 1 enables "Console Sharing" on a different PS5, Player 2 loses access.
  • PS Plus: If Player 1 has PS Plus, Player 2 can play Player 1's games online and access their PS Plus monthly games.

Trust is key. Player 1 needs internet for their own games after setup. For digital games only.
